--------------------------------------------------
EV BOARD (BOARD 1) – 6 MATRIX AUTO FILL
--------------------------------------------------
Entry Amount: ₹5900

Split:
- ₹900 → GST
- ₹3900 → Company Account
- ₹500 → Direct Sponsor Income (Main Wallet)
- ₹150 × 4 levels → Level Income (Rebirth Wallet)

Placement Logic:
- 6x matrix (auto-fill)
- First come, first fill
- Spillover enabled
- If sponsor’s 6 slots are not filled, new users are placed under sponsor
- After sponsor’s matrix is full, placement spills to downline

Reward Condition:
- Once Level 1 (6 users) + Level 2 (36 users) are completed
- AND user has directly referred **6 users**
→ User can **claim an EV vehicle worth ₹1,00,000 for free**

--------------------------------------------------
REBIRTH LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------
- Level income goes to **Rebirth Wallet**
- When Rebirth Wallet reaches ₹5900 for the first time:
  → User automatically enters **Silver Board**
- After first rebirth:
  - Every ₹5900 accumulation creates a **new rebirth ID**
  - Direct sponsor income of rebirth IDs goes to **Mother ID**
- Any rebirth created inside rebirth accounts is **company-controlled**
- Users cannot access company rebirth accounts

--------------------------------------------------
SILVER BOARD (BOARD 2)
--------------------------------------------------
Entry: From EV Board accumulated ₹5900

Split:
- ₹900 → GST
- ₹500 distributed across 6 levels (jungle method)
- Excess → Company

Placement:
- Jungle method
- Example:
  - John has users: 1–6
  - 7 placed under 1
  - 8 under 2
  - 9 under 3
  - After all users receive first placement, second placement starts
  - Continues level by level

Withdrawal Rule:
- First ₹10,000 earned is **locked**
- ₹10,000 is auto-used to enter **Gold Board**
- After Gold entry, Silver income becomes withdrawable
